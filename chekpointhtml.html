                                                                                          About Design Graphic

   Graphic design is visual communication put into practice. It's about using images to convey information and concepts to an audience. But it's also a pretty nebulous term, and depending on who you're talking to, it can totally vary. Some use the term to refer exclusively to analogous (or printed) design products. Some also use the term to describe almost any other discipline containing the word "design." And for still others, graphic design can even encompass all art. But what is graphic design?
